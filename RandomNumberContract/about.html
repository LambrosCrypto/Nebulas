Randon Number

Getting a random number between a minimum and a maximum, powered by Nebulas Blockchain.
This is a free service, only pay a small transaction fee.

How-to:

  • Enter a minimum number.
  • Enter a maximum number.
  • Click 'Request Number'.
  • You'll be redirected to a link you can share with the selected number between the minimum and maximum (both included) you just selected (once the transaction completes).

Example:

I want to do a giveaway on Twitter. Unless people watch me do the entire process live, they will suspect cheating. We need to know that the number was randomly selected and that the order of the list was not modified after the fact. By posting the 'data', you can use this service for any use case requiring a verifiable random selection - allowing people to manually confirm it was done fairly.

Requirements

Anyone can view a random number selected with this tool. In order to request a new random number, you will need $nas for gas and the WebExtensionWallet available here: https://github.com/ChengOrangeJu/WebExtensionWallet
